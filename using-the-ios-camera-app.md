# Using the iOS Camera app

1. In the Camera app, tap and drag select Video to set it as capture mode.
2. To focus the iPad/ iPhone on a particular person or object, tap that area on the screen.
3. To zoom in or out, touch the screen with two fingers and pinch expand the viewing area. (You can also use the slider at the bottom of the screen to do this.)
4. Tap the red record button to begin filming. When you're finished filming, tap the red record button again. All of your footage will automatically save to the iOS Photos app. 
5. To exit the Camera app, press the Home button on the iPad/ iPhone.
